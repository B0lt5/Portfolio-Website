My First Post: Why I'm Learning to Code

KT • August 31, 2025

A short introduction to my journey as a web developer and my passion for problem-solving with code.

From a young age, I’ve always been fascinated by how things work. Whether it was taking apart an old toy or solving a word puzzle, I found joy in deconstructing a problem to understand how it works. This curiosity is what first led me to the world of web development.

As an aspiring web developer, my goal is to build clean, functional, and user-friendly websites. I am dedicated to learning new technologies and improving my skills every day. This blog will be a place where I document my journey, share what I've learned, and connect with others who are on a similar path.

Thank you for joining me on this journey!
